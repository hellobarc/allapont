4. Integrales - Ejercicio 31.1.a

Calcular: \( \displaystyle \int_1^2 \Big(\frac{1}{t}-i\Big)^2 dt\)

Prueba:

\( \begin{aligned} \displaystyle \int_1^2 \Big(\frac{1}{t}-i\Big)^2 dt &= \displaystyle \int_1^2 \Big( \frac{1}{t} \Big)^2-2i\frac{1}{t}+i^2 dt \\ &= \displaystyle \int_1^2 t^{-2} dt - 2i\int_1^2 \frac{1}{t} dt - \int_1^2 1 dt \\ &= \displaystyle -t^{-1} \biggm\vert_1^2 - 2i \ln{(t)} \biggm\vert_1^2 - t \biggm\vert_1^2 \\ &= \displaystyle -2^{-1} \cancel{+1} - 2i \ln{(2)} \cancel{-1} \\ \end{aligned} \)

\(\displaystyle \therefore \int_1^2 \Big(\frac{1}{t}-i\Big)^2 dt = -2^{-1} - 2i \ln{(2)}\)
